Welcome to the King Music Studio Intonation Project!

Instructions:

You can play the piano! Unfortunately, it's just a sine wave, and you can't play multiple notes yet :(

If you want to adjust the temperament, you can move the slider side to side. Listen to the piano detune!

To start tuning, click "Start Tuning!" and it will turn on your microphone.

Sing/play a note, and the tuner will start documenting how sharp or flat you are in .5 second intervals, you will see this as the tone is heard. It will automatically scroll with each sample.

The piano will also highlight what note the microphone is picking up so you know exactly where you are.

Because this is unfinished, you can't submit the information, but as I develop this, you'll see more progress!
