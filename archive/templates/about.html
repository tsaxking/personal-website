About Me

Hello! I’m Taylor Reese King. I am a 4th year Music Composition major at Boise State University, and I’m also working on their new Arts Entrepreneurship minor! Starting on piano at age six, I picked up the alto saxophone at 10, and began composing at 13. At 16, I started teaching saxophone, and offering composition lessons since 19. Since 2020, I've started studying sound engineering, and built a home recording studio. I apply this practice in lessons to help students visualize what they're playing.

I have written over 20 compositions, with seven having been performed or recorded. You can see several here! I was on a robotics team in high school and now mentor the team in mechanical design and building. Recently, I've developed a love for computer science. One thing I'm proud of in that field combining music and programming is this!

Whatever my future holds, I know music will be a part of it!
