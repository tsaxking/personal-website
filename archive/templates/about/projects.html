Film Scoring

In my film scoring class with Dr. Alexander, one of our assignments was to score the docking scene for the film Interstellar as if we were hired to do it.

I think I'm going to do more of these in the future and post them on my youtube channel in the playlist embedded below! It was quite fun, but some may have mixed feelings because these were already scored by some of the greatest composers out there. This is mostly for fun and to showcase my compositional style to those who are looking for a film composer!

Website

This whole website was built by me without a website editor like Wix or Wordpress!

I started to learn how to program with javascript in december of 2020 when I built the Boise State University Library Database Worksheet. This led me down a path of learning and brought me to CodeAcademy!

This project originally started with my friend Bear, we were going to do a join business venture called "Moffett King Studio," but our schedules could not align well enough to do much with it so we decided to do independent ventures, hence "King Music Studio." Regardless, it was a lot of fun and I learned a lot in the process!
